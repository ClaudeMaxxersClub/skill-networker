## Message Archetypes

### Tribal Opener

Shared context plus genuine curiosity plus low-friction ask.

### Evidence Opener

Specific thing they said plus useful reaction plus small ask.

### Mutual Intro

Mutual's name plus why the connection is useful plus one sentence ask.
