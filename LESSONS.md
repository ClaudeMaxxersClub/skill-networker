# Lessons

## Local Knowledge Often Changes the Path

A target that looks cold externally may already be a customer, former lead,
event attendee, or second-degree connection. Load local paths before external
research when configured.

## Shared Institution Is Not Enough

Same school five years apart is weak. Same program and same year is strong.
Compute temporal overlap for every bridge.

## Fetch Before Citing

Search results are leads. Dossiers cite fetched pages and local files only.

## "Know But Don't Say" Prevents Creepy Outreach

Some facts help avoid mistakes but should not be referenced directly. Keep them
out of messages unless they are plainly professional and non-sensitive.

## Confirmed Mutuals Beat Clever Inference

When Sales Navigator or CRM data arrives, re-rank warm paths around confirmed
mutuals instead of preserving speculative bridges.
