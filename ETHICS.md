# Ethics and Scope

Networker is for legitimate professional networking, sales preparation,
partnership evaluation, investor/partner diligence, and meeting preparation.

## Allowed

- Public company websites
- Public professional profiles
- Public talks, podcasts, interviews, articles, conference bios
- Public corporate registries, funding databases, filings, grants, patents
- User-owned local notes, CRM exports, and prior dossiers
- Public social posts when relevant to professional context

## Not Allowed

- minors
- private individuals with no public professional footprint
- private home addresses or property records
- private family data
- real-time location tracking
- private fitness/check-in traces
- authenticated scraping
- paywall bypasses
- coercive leverage, embarrassing personal facts, rumors, or unverified accusations
- religion, politics, nationality, wealth, family status, or immigration inferences from name or appearance

## Handling Sensitive Public Facts

Some public facts are not outreach-safe. Put them in `Know But Don't Say` only
when they materially affect respectful communication. Omit them entirely if they
create pressure, embarrassment, or coercive leverage.

## Confidence Rules

- `HIGH`: multiple independent sources or a primary source
- `MEDIUM`: one credible source
- `LOW`: evidence-backed inference; never state as fact

If confidence is low, phrase as a hypothesis or leave it out of outreach.
